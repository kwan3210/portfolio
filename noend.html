Description

NoEnd. is a project initiated by the co-founder of an influencer marketing platform, Scrunch. The project is a mobile app that helps people invest time into themselves through daily habits. NoEnd. will be launched in the first half of 2020. 

Process


My role: UX/UI Designer (Contract)

  • Creating a functional prototype
  • Refining product UI
  • Phases: Design + Prototype
  • Platforms: Mobile

The project was already in the final design stage hence, my job was to refining current UI in Adobe XD to ensure they look polished and smart enough for users to navigate the app without confusion. After the refinement, the designs were imported into Supernova, which is a software that converts mobile app designs into native frontend code.
