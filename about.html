About

Hi, I'm Elliot Kim.

I'm a UX designer based in Brisbane. I use human-centered design to create beautiful user experiences. Understanding the human experience is essential for creating useful and effective products. I enjoy using my skill set to empower people to accomplish their goals. I create digital experiences that make life easier. I also love creating graphic arts and illustrations using Adobe Creative Suite. Most of my artworks are inspired by video games and Korean popular culture.
